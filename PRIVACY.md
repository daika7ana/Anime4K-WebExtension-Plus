# Privacy Policy

**Anime4K WebExtension Plus**

Last updated: June 2026

## Overview

Anime4K WebExtension Plus is a video enhancement tool that improves anime video quality using real-time super-resolution algorithms. This extension is designed with privacy in mind — **no user data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third parties**.

## Data Collection

**This extension does not collect any user data.** Specifically:

- No personal information is gathered
- No browsing history is recorded
- No usage statistics or analytics are collected
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used

## Local Data Storage

The extension stores settings locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API. This data **never leaves your device** and is used solely to provide extension functionality:

### Synced Settings
These settings sync across your devices via your browser account and include:
- **Selected enhancement mode** — your preferred video enhancement preset
- **Target resolution** — your chosen output resolution (e.g., 2x, 4K)
- **Whitelist rules** — websites where the extension is active
- **Custom modes** — user-created enhancement configurations
- **Cross-origin fix preference** — compatibility setting for certain video players

### Local Settings
These settings remain only on the current device:
- **Performance tier** — your hardware performance level (detected via benchmark)
- **GPU benchmark results** — graphics card performance score
- **Onboarding status** — whether initial setup has been completed

## Browser Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions, used exclusively for video enhancement:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your preferences and settings locally |
| `tabs` | Detect video elements on web pages |
| `scripting` | Inject video enhancement shaders |
| `declarativeNetRequest` | Fix cross-origin video playback issues |
| `host_permissions` | Access video elements on websites you visit |

**None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or share user data.**

## Network Activity

This extension **does not make any network requests** to external servers. All video processing is performed locally within your browser using WebGPU technology.

## Third-Party Services

No data is shared with any third-party services, advertisers, analytics providers, or other external entities.

## Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there are no data security risks associated with external data transmission. Your settings are stored securely within your browser's storage system.

## User Control

You have full control over your data:
- **View settings**: Access all stored data through the extension's options page
- **Modify settings**: Change any preference at any time
- **Delete data**: Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data
- **Whitelist control**: Choose which websites the extension operates on

## Offline Mode

The extension operates entirely offline. All video enhancement is performed locally using your device's GPU. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any information from children under 13 years of age, or from any other user.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's update notes on the relevant browser extension store listing.
